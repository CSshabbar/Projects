Hi, my name is Mohammed Shabbar, and I’m an AI machine learning enthusiast in the making. I’ve always had a fascination with Artificial Intelligence, mainly because of how it can reshape industries and solve complex problems in ways we never thought possible. I love building AI-powered solutions, especially when they can make a real impact.
